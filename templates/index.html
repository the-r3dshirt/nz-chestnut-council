NEW ZEALAND CHESTNUT COUNCIL INC.

The New Zealand Chestnut Council Inc (NZCC) is the nationally recognised product group for chestnuts.

The aim of NZCC is to encourage, promote and advance New Zealand's Chestnut Industry including the promotion of the sale and consumption, of fresh and processed New Zealand grown Chestnuts, both in New Zealand and overseas, for the benefit of New Zealand Chestnut Growers.

If you wish to join the NZ Chestnut Council and receive the NZCC "Chestnutz News" newsletter (electronic sample available on request ) , please pay the annual NZCC fee of $130.00.
